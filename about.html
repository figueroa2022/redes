MISSION

The primary design that stimulated the origin of NETSAFE, is to provide a professional IT service committed and highly responsible to small and medium enterprises, providing the confidence, security and peace of mind necessary both to manage and maintain their technology park and to to acquire quality solutions at a reasonable price, in a complex world that evolves at the speed of light, such as new technologies.

VIEW

NETSAFE is anxious to become a provider of recognized computer security services and reference worldwide, being a great super company consistent and in constant development, committed to technological safety and social development of the community, primarily preserving the environment.

OBJECTIVES

Improve the satisfaction of its customers and suppliers, the professional and personal development of its employees and the social commitment, motivating the creation of solid relationships with all parties based on fundamental ethical principles.
